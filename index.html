About This Website

This Community Health Reporting platform is designed for quick, anonymous reporting of health symptoms and cases in your area. It empowers communities and health workers to monitor trends, spot outbreaks early, and respond faster. All reports are stored locally in your browser for privacy and demo purposes. Try submitting a report and see it instantly on the dashboard!
